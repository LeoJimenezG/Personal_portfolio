Main Projects

Tamay: Cuidado y bienestar de las plantas domésticas


A mobile app designed to track and care for your domestic plants. My role involved creating APIs to retrieve, process, and send weather information, as well as providing tailored recommendations based on that data to enhance the app's functionality. I developed these APIs using the Python Flask framework and successfully integrated them with the app's backend to ensure seamless communication between the mobile application and the weather data services.

Modelo Logístico para predecir a tiempo la deserción escolar


This project was particularly meaningful due to its objective: predicting student dropout possibility in a timely manner using a Logistic Model. My contributions included using R to clean and preprocess the data, as well as to train and test the model. Additionally, I took the lead in writing, sharing, and explaining most of the project's documentation. This process allowed me to deeply explore the mathematics behind the model and its foundational principles, enhancing my understanding and involvement in the project.
